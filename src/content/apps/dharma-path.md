Dharma Path brings the essential texts and rituals of daily devotion into one calm,
modern app. Browse a growing library of scriptures, follow along with Aarti and
Chalisa, keep track of festivals with a rich calendar, and check the daily
Panchang — all designed to work offline, without clutter or distraction.

The app is built to feel respectful and unobtrusive: comfortable typography,
adjustable text sizes, and a distraction-free reader put the focus entirely on
the content. Everything you save stays on your device, and the core library is
available whether or not you have a connection.
